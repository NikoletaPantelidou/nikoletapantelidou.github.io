Hey! I am a PhD student at the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona and active member at the Centre de Lingüística Teòrica. My research lies at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Computational Linguistics, and cognitive approaches to language, with a particular focus on exploring and explaining the linguistic abilities of Large Language Models (LLMs). Prior to starting my PhD, I received my Master’s Degree in Cognitive Science and Language from the Universitat de Barcelona. Previously, I completed my Bachelor’s Degree in the field of English Language and Literature at the . During that period, I was awarded the International Student Award by the Republic of Cyprus for obtaining the highest entrance grade among international students in the School of Humanities. I am currently supported by an FPI fellowship, a competitive predoctoral contract awarded for doctoral research by the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation.
